What is Apostille?

Apostille is a type of document authentication issued to documents for use in countries that participate in the Hague Convention of 1961. This certification simplifies the process of using official documents abroad such as birth certificates, marriage certificates, educational degrees, and commercial documents. Whether you're planning to study, work, get married, or move overseas, Apostille is a legal necessity.
